# Business Analytics & Data Mining Portfolio

This repository contains a collection of end-to-end analytics and data
mining projects completed as part of a graduate-level Business Analytics
program. The projects demonstrate the application of analytics techniques
to real-world business and operational datasets.

---

## Skills Demonstrated
- Business Intelligence & Dashboarding (SAP Analytics Cloud)
- ERP & Sales Analytics
- Data Mining & Pattern Discovery
- Clustering & Association Rule Mining
- Time Series Forecasting
- Regression & Predictive Modeling
- Audit & Anomaly Detection

---

## Tools & Technologies
- SAP Analytics Cloud
- Microsoft Excel
- Statistical & Analytical Modeling Techniques
- Data Visualization & KPI Reporting
